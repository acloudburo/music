    Jamiroquai - Corner of the Earth (Live in Verona)

    90ties

    Posted on Feb 13



    “Corner of the Earth” is the fourth and final single from British funk/acid jazz band Jamiroquai’s fifth studio album, A Funk Odyssey. The song was written by Jason Kay and Rob Harris and is a bossa nova-type track, reflecting on the problems that people of the Earth have to suffer. The song peaked at #31 on the UK Singles Chart and was the last Jamiroquai single to use the DVD format. (Source: Wikipedia)
